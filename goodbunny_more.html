TheGoodBunnyClub Animation

This animation rendition for the fashion brand TheGoodBunnyClub created by Alice Yuen portray's her struggle to assimilate into society as someone with Aspergers. Similar to how the broken bunnies are labelled as broken, they are tossed out of sight. However, in the darkness these souls awaken proving that they are still conscious, have feelings, and have a right to live free of social expectations and judgement.

Here is a transparent setup of the environment. Inside is the bottomless pit of unwanted bunnies deemed useless by social pressures.

Once the hatch closes the blanket of darkness covers their faces. Naturally darkness is a place of comfort, and here they finally awaken.

Moments before the hatch is closed, we see the lifeless heads lay endlessly.

The bunny shaped head as both a poly and smooth asset.
